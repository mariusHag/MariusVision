My First Semester Project

in September 2025 I Started my bachellor in Architechture at the royal Academy. This is the first Project I have done Studying architechture.

the theme was timber framing. And my Program was a room for hygiene

the purpose of the semester was to become familiaer with the tectonics of wood, med udgangspunkt i timberframing. Developing at first, simple cardboard folded models, and then timber frame models following the same shape

We were then challenged to juxtapose at least two timberframed models. and within an axonometric drawing, Explore the spatial opportunities these mismatching shapes would create.

to consolidate this concept we had to create a 1/25 scale model of the house. Here my focus was mainly to further explore and play with the spatial opportunities.

I wanted to work with the bathhouse as something mysterious, imagining the blurred silhouettes of people bathing behind glass brick walls. The contour of a stair leading up to a large thatched roof adds to the atmosphere, raising questions about what might be above.
